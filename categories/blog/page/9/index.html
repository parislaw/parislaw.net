Pray

Prayer has power. If you are feeling out of tune, Pray. This will give you the space to reset. If you feel there are too many things to do and not enough time, pray. This will help remind you to focus on what’s important. If you feel nervous about something coming up, or you mind is stuck in something happening now, pray. This will make you more present. If you feel things are moving too fast, pray. This will slow you down. If you feel excited and an achievement, pray. We are able to do these things through God’s wisdom and guidance. In all things, give thanks. In all circumstances, pray. Prayer has power. Prayer is an opportunity for us to reconnect with God and get back in resonance with our true purpose. When in tune our worries, doubts, and fears all fizzle away. Then we can get back to doing extraordinary things and having joy while doing them. ...
