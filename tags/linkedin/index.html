Watch me work - rewriting linkedin profile summary draft part II

I am back with another draft of my linkedin summary. If you missed it, go check out part one. My reflection I am structuring the summary the right way, but it doesn’t feel like it is completely there yet in terms of having that solid umph. You know what I mean? Writing that first, mouthwatering, juicy-steak type opening will take some time I think. I will have to eventually just decide on a draft and then evolve it over time. Why write multiple drafts? Writing multiple drafts allows you to articulate your message in different ways. You can play around with a variety of angles and then pick and choose the parts that resonate well with you. Don’t forget to follow along by downloading the presentation and worksheet. They will help get into details. It may help to go back to see my starting point.
